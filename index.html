FAQ · 06

Frequently
asked.

How much does it cost? +

Diagnostic call: free. Two-week pilot: from USD 3,500. Full program (3 to 5 processes, 6 to 10 weeks): from USD 12,000. Fixed price agreed before we start. No usage fees, no escalating invoices.

What about sensitive data? Does it go to OpenAI? +

It depends. For public data (Official Gazette, INPI, AFIP) we use commercial APIs with no-training clauses. For case files, contracts or client data we run local models with zero egress. We sign an NDA and the architecture stays documented and auditable. If compliance requires it, we route you to Sovereign (dedicated infrastructure).

Why not just use ChatGPT directly? +

A chat helps you draft. A pipeline does the work without anyone copy-pasting and reviewing. It runs by itself, integrates with your systems, audits results, and scales without adding headcount. The difference shows up on your invoices.

What if I already have an internal developer? +

Even better. We work with your team, not around it. The goal of the program is for your developer to maintain and extend the pipeline after we leave. We hand off clean code, documented, with tests where they matter.

Who owns the code? +

You do, in a repo of yours, from day one. No vendor lock-in, no "you have to pay me to access your own system". You can continue solo, switch providers, or take the pipeline anywhere.

What happens if the pilot does not work? +

If we miss the agreed milestone in the agreed timeframe, the next sprint runs on our account. We would rather say no to a project than promise something we cannot deliver.

Do you work with companies outside Argentina? +

Yes. 100% remote operation, focused on Latin America and US time zones. The stack runs on any modern cloud (AWS, GCP, Azure) or on-prem.

Privacy · 07

Your data is not our data.

Standard NDA

We sign an NDA before we see your data. The architecture is documented and auditable by you or your security team.

Local models when they matter

Case files, contracts and sensitive data are processed on-prem or on dedicated infrastructure. No egress to third-party APIs.

Commercial APIs: no-training

When we use OpenAI, Anthropic or Google, we always do it under contracts that ban training on your data. No exceptions.

Law 25.326 compatible

Personal data handled under Argentina's Personal Data Protection Law. Logs, retention policies and right-to-be-forgotten when applicable.
